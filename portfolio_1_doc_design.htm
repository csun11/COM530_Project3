  • Brochure for project result demonstration event (i.e. IPRO Day at IIT) PDF file

    IPRO day is the showtime for tens of IIT student teams, who have spent 4-5 months working on their Inter-Professional projects. It is surely a festivity for document designers, too! At the exhibition booths with brochures and posters flying around, they can enjoy handing out or hanging up their masterpieces, and can indulge themselves with appreciating or criticizing what others have crafted.

    Here are what my team used for showcasing our consulting work with Sloan Valve Company. I was so much honored to be given the task of designing both the poster and the brochure; also, to my great pleasure, our presentation won the highest score among 5 teams in the same track.
