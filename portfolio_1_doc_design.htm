    You may have noticed that the name of the client here, "Trail's End Rehabilitation and Retirement Farm," is just a pseudonym; however, to make sure that the design could properly prompt this kind of business, I almost granted myself a excuse holiday dedicated for horse riding.

    By the way, You are invited to take this little quiz, after looking at the brochure carefully:

    When designing the color scheme, I was thinking from the perspective of a horse, who was recognized by his peer horses as an expert in Science of Food Preservation. So, please tell which of the brochure's panels are outside and which are inside.

  • Brochure and poster for demonstrating results from a consulting project

    IPRO day is the showtime for tens of IIT student teams, who have spent 4-5 months working on their Inter-Professional projects. It is surely a festivity for document designers, too! At the exhibition booths with brochures and posters flying around, they can enjoy handing out or hanging up their masterpieces, and can indulge themselves with appreciating or criticizing what others have crafted.

    Here are what my team used for showcasing our consulting work with Sloan Valve Company. I was so much honored to be given the task of designing both the poster and the brochure; also, to my great pleasure, our presentation won the highest score among 5 teams in the same track.
